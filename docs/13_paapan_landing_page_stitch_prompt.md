# Prompt Google Stitch: Landing Page Paapan

Gunakan prompt di bawah ini untuk membuat landing page Paapan di Google Stitch.

---

## Prompt Utama

Design a modern landing page for **Paapan**, an AI-powered visual canvas workspace.

The landing page should feel **simple, emotional, spacious, soft, and premium**, with a strong sense of **open sky, breathing room, and clarity**. The visual direction should be inspired by a **sky gradient background**, soft clouds, airy spacing, rounded cards, subtle glassmorphism, and a calm white interface. Avoid looking too corporate or too much like a finance SaaS dashboard. This should feel like a product for thinking, creativity, and visual AI work.

## Core Product Meaning

Paapan was created from a specific frustration:

- Traditional AI chat interfaces are vertical and make old context sink too far upward.
- Important ideas become hard to find again.
- Images, notes, and AI conversations are separated instead of living together.
- People need a way to think visually with AI, not just chat line by line.

Paapan exists to solve that.

It is a visual AI canvas where users can:

- place ideas on a canvas
- connect thoughts visually
- use AI chat bubbles directly inside the workspace
- let AI understand nearby context on the canvas
- let AI read surrounding notes, linked nodes, and images
- revisit old thinking without losing context in a long vertical chat history

This product should not be positioned as “just another whiteboard”.
It should feel like:

**“A visual thinking space where AI, notes, images, and connections live together.”**

## Main Goal of the Landing Page

The page should communicate:

1. Why Paapan exists
2. Why vertical AI chat is not enough for deep thinking
3. Why a visual canvas with AI is a better way to explore ideas
4. A strong invitation to try the product

## Layout Direction

Create a landing page with only **2 major visual sections**.

### Section 1: Hero

Include:

- top navigation
- Paapan logo or wordmark
- simple nav items
- primary CTA button
- large hero headline
- supporting paragraph
- primary CTA and secondary CTA
- a large product preview/mockup

The hero should feel elegant, centered, premium, and airy.

Suggested tone:

- thoughtful
- creative
- calm
- spacious
- human

The product preview should show:

- a canvas-like workspace
- floating AI chat bubbles
- connected notes or nodes
- a few linked ideas
- image context inside the workspace
- not a typical vertical chat UI

## Section 2: Why Paapan Exists

This section should feel like a meaningful product story, not just feature blocks.

Include:

- a short “why we made this” narrative
- the frustration with vertical AI chat interfaces
- the benefit of visual thinking with connected context
- 3 or 4 simple value points
- a compact closing CTA

Possible value points:

- AI that understands nearby context
- Ideas stay visible instead of sinking away
- Notes, images, and prompts live in one place
- Better for brainstorming, mapping, and long-form thinking

## Copy Direction

Use concise, clear, emotionally intelligent product copy.
Do not sound overly salesy.
Do not use generic startup buzzwords.
The copy should feel like it comes from a real frustration and a real product insight.

## Suggested Headline Directions

Use one of these directions or create something similar:

- AI that thinks with you on a canvas, not inside a sinking chat
- A visual AI workspace for ideas that need space
- Where notes, images, and AI can finally think together
- Stop losing context in vertical AI chat
- Think with AI in a space you can actually see

## Suggested Supporting Copy Direction

Paapan was built for people who are tired of AI chats that bury old context.
Organize ideas on a visual canvas, connect notes and images, and let AI respond with awareness of the space around it.

## Style Requirements

- Sky blue gradient background
- Soft clouds or atmospheric depth
- Rounded white container
- Subtle blur and soft shadows
- Minimal but premium navigation
- Large typography
- Gentle contrast, mostly light theme
- Clean modern interface
- Avoid purple-heavy startup aesthetics
- Avoid dark mode
- Avoid clutter

## UX Requirements

- Mobile responsive
- Desktop-first composition
- Clear CTA hierarchy
- Readable text contrast
- Strong visual focus on the hero message and product preview

## Deliverable

Create a polished landing page concept that feels ready for a modern AI product launch.
The final result should feel like a calm, premium, emotionally resonant landing page for a visual AI canvas product.

---

## Versi Singkat

Kalau ingin prompt yang lebih singkat, pakai ini:

Design a 2-section landing page for **Paapan**, a visual AI canvas product. The page should feel spacious, calm, premium, and emotionally meaningful, with a soft **sky gradient** background, airy layout, rounded white containers, subtle shadows, and a clean modern interface. Paapan was created because traditional **vertical AI chat** makes old context sink and become hard to revisit. This product lets users think with AI on a **canvas**, where notes, images, connected ideas, and AI chat bubbles live together in one visual space. The landing page should explain **why Paapan exists**, show a large hero with a canvas-style product preview, and include a second story-driven section about the frustration with vertical AI chat and the value of visual connected thinking. Avoid generic SaaS dashboard vibes, avoid dark mode, and avoid overly salesy copy.

---

## Catatan Tambahan

- Jika Stitch memberi hasil terlalu mirip SaaS dashboard biasa, tekan lagi arah “visual thinking product” dan “canvas with AI bubbles”.
- Jika hasilnya terlalu ramai, minta spacing lebih lega dan kurangi elemen sekunder.
- Jika hasilnya terlalu generik, minta lebih menonjolkan narasi “vertical chat buries old context”.
